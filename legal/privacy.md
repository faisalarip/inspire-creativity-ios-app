# Privacy Policy — InspireCreativity

_Last updated: June 8, 2026_

InspireCreativity ("the app", "we", "us") is an iOS app that lets you browse and
copy hand-crafted SwiftUI animation code. This policy explains exactly what we
collect, why, and your choices. We designed the app to collect as little as
possible.

## What we collect

**Account information (only if you create an account).**
Creating an account is optional — you can browse the entire catalog and purchase
Pro without one. If you do sign up, we collect:

- **Email address** — used to authenticate you, send verification and
  password-reset emails, and recover your account. It is not used for
  advertising and is never sold.
- **Name (first and last)** — used to personalize your account. It is not used
  for advertising and is never sold.
- **A user identifier** — assigned by our authentication provider to associate
  your account with your sign-in.

Authentication is handled by **Supabase** (supabase.com), acting as our data
processor. Your email and credentials are stored on Supabase infrastructure.

**Purchases.**
The Lifetime unlock (a one-time purchase) is processed entirely by **Apple** via
the App Store. We never receive or store your payment-card details. Apple shares
only the transaction/entitlement information needed to unlock Pro on your
devices.

**On-device data.**
Favorites and basic preferences are stored locally on your device using
`UserDefaults`. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

## What we do NOT do

- We do **not** track you across other apps or websites.
- We do **not** use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
- We do **not** sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

## How we use your information

We use the limited information above only to: operate your account, verify your
email, let you reset your password, deliver your Pro entitlement, and respond to
support requests.

## Data retention and deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from **Settings → Delete
account** inside the app. This removes your account and associated authentication
data from our systems. You may also email us to request deletion.

## Children

InspireCreativity is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly
collect personal information from them.

## Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last
updated" date above.
